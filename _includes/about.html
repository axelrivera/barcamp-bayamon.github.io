What's this all about?

BarCamp is an ad-hoc unconference born from the desire for people to share and learn in an open environment.
It is an intense event with discussions, demos and interaction from attendees.
All presentations are scheduled the day they happen.
Prepare in advance, but come early to get a slot on the wall.
The people present at the event will select the demos or presentations they want to see.

Community And Information

Anyone with something to contribute or with the desire to learn is welcome and invited to join.

No Spectators, only participants

Attendees must give a demo, a session, or help with one, or otherwise volunteer / contribute in some way to support the event.

Share, Share, Share

Be prepared to share with everybody at the event!
